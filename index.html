About RVR

Built on Integrity.
Delivered with Precision.

RVR Building Projects has been crafting exceptional homes across the Knysna Garden Route for over 15 years. We bring the same level of care, craftsmanship, and accountability to every project — whether it's a full new construction, a considered renovation, or a seamless addition.

Our founder Rayo leads every project personally, ensuring that the standard we promise is the standard you receive. From first consultation to final handover, we hold ourselves to a single benchmark: excellence.
